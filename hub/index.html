Three SaaS Opportunities

Simple tools for recurring nonprofit paperwork

Less is more: each concept starts with one clear output, human review, and reusable approved language.

01

Grant and Report Assistant

Draft grant answers, progress reports, funder updates, and reusable program narratives from approved notes and past language.

  • Best wedge: immediate pain and visible time savings.
  • First demo: paste notes and get a reviewed draft.
02

Board Packet Assistant

Create board agendas, minutes, ED updates, decision logs, and follow-up lists without rebuilding every packet manually.

  • Best wedge: repeatable monthly workflow.
  • First demo: meeting notes to board-ready packet.
03

Nonprofit Knowledge Desk

Help staff find trusted answers across policies, prior grants, board materials, reports, and meeting decisions.

  • Best wedge: preserve institutional memory.
  • First demo: ask a question and cite approved sources.
